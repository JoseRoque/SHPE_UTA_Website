Why Join?

  • Discounted registration to SHPE National, Regional & Local events
  • Complimentary SHPE stoles upon graduation
  • Complimentary subscriptions to SHPE Magazine and Hispanic Business Magazine
  • Eligible to nominate or be nominated for SHPE's most prestigious national awards
